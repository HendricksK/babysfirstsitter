About us

Baby's First Sitter is a babysitting company providing professional childcare to families across the Western Cape. Catering for any need, whether it be a two hour babysit while you go shopping, or a two day sleep over sit while you celebrate your anniversary- whatever the need, we provide safe, reliable, and reputable child care.

About the owner

Naomi Groenewald has been all about kids since the age of 15. With experience in both South Africa, and the United States of America, she has gained a great wealth of knowledge related to children in her nearly 10 years of child care work. It is with this passion and experience that she runs a company committed to providing hundreds of families across the Western Cape with childcare of the highest standard.
